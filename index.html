Mission

My mission in life is not to live a calm life. I want it to be a vociferous one. I belive that, by taking up challenges, life can really be a colorful one. In this short life of 25 years since I've born on December 12,1994, I saw a lot of people taking up challenges often in their lives and be happy. On the other hand, I've also seen a lot of people not taking up challenges and live their lives in discontentment.

So, to me living a happy life doesn't mean to survive in this world calmly even with great luxury! Instead of that, I like myself to be challenged and live a life full of adventure which exactly a web developer has.

That's why I've made my mind to be a web developer and lead a challenging life full of joy and adventure of creating something new using my logic and creativity.

It can also be said in another way that I love creating new things and that's exactly what I can do by being a web developer and so, my mission is to become a web developer.

Vision

To be exact, I want to change the world by doing something extraordinary with something which I have pation and interest on. And by doing so I want to make the world a better one for living.
